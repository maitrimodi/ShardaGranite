Granite is a durable, elegant igneous rock ideal for construction and design. Used in countertops, flooring, and monuments, Indian granite stands out for its vibrant colors and quality, adding timeless beauty and strength to any space.

G20 Black

This is another type of Black granite from South India. The color varies from medium to dark black with a few specks on it which can be viewed if taken a closer look. This is extremely good in areas with a high footfall and are a wonderful choice for kitchen countertops and tabletops too.

Black Galaxy

Black Galaxy granite is very popular form of Indian granite found in quarries of South India. It has a pitch-dark black background with golden and silver speckles which gives it a look of a starry night sky. The Black Galaxy has very even patterns and can be used for both indoors and outdoors and is ideal for ﬂooring, countertops and tabletops.

Steel Grey

Steel Grey is a form of Indian granite originating from South India. It comes with a light to dark background of Grey with ﬂowers of lighter shades that look like pearls. Steel Grey has a very consistent color with minor variations in texture. This color is suited for both indoor and outdoor use and can be used for ﬂooring, cladding and countertops.

TAN BROWN WC

This granite, quarried in South India, has complex and interesting patterns. When seen from a distance, this stone is clearly a two- toned granite and has both black and brown minerals which make them irresistible to the eyes. The brown spots can range from almost metallic copper brown in color through to dark chocolate brown in some sections.

Vizag Blue

Vizag Blue is a granite found in Andhra Pradesh, South India. It has a blue background with a lot of movements of grey and white veins and is also called Blue Granite. It looks best as kitchen and bathroom countertops.

Lavender Blue

These are quarried in Eastern part of India. They have a blue background with dark waves and have burgundy/purple specks on them. From far, they look lavender in color. They are very hard and can be used for countertops, ﬂoorings and even wall claddings. They are of great demand in many countries.

New Himalayan Blue

Himalayan Blue is found in Karnataka, South India. This comes with a brownish blue background and has orange swirls and streaks. As it has low water absorption, it is ideal for both indoor and outdoor applications.

Hassan Green

This granite is found in South India and has medium grains on it. It has a blackish green background with lighter green specks on it. It looks extremely attractive and is very durable. They can be used as countertops and ﬂoorings. They also look best when used for staircases and wall claddings.

Ivory Brown

Ivory Brown Granite is found in South India, It is a stunning natural stone featuring a warm ivory base with flowing brown and beige veins. Its elegant patterns and soft tones make it ideal for countertops, flooring, and wall cladding, adding a sophisticated touch to any space.

Astoria Ivory

Astoria Ivory is quarried in South India. It is predominantly a creamy golden color granite which captures the eye from afar. This golden color can range all the way from a slightly oﬀ-white color – to a color that resembles wet sand. The creamy golden background has dark brown spots and veins. They can be used as countertops, wall claddings and for ﬂoorings.

Colonial White

Colonial White granite, also known as Millennium Cream, features white to ivory patterns with purple and dark grey specks. Colonial White has natural earthy tones that makes brushed ﬁnish a desirable option for people looking to create a more natural look in their home. Having a light base, the stone proves to be the perfect choice for those who would want to add class and elegance to their space.

Colonial Gold

Colonial Gold is a stunning cream and white stone that is loved for its golden tones. It is quarried in Tamil Nadu, South India. The stone is littered with small mineral deposits called quartz that range from a very light to medium brown. These stunning little crystals add shine and sparkle to the stone when exposed to natural light. Colonial Gold is a good choice of stone for ﬂoorings and kitchen. countertops and tabletops.

New Indian Titanium

Black granite has its own class. This stunning black granite comes with white, grey and fawn swirls that give it a bold appeal which will make anyone who sees it sigh in awe. The New Indian Titanium is highly durable and hence can be used in areas with a high footfall. It is ideal for kitchen countertops and bathroom vanity tops.

Black Forest

Black Forest is found in Andhra Pradesh, South India. This granite has dark background with white waves. Its charm and elegance will look splendid in any given space and is so durable that it can easily last for decades without the need for repolish. This granite is mostly used for kitchen countertops, wall tiles and ﬂooring.

Imperial Gold

This granite is quarried in South India. It comes with a yellow and gold background and has the greatest number of variations in pattern. It has white, black and grey veins throughout and some specks of darker gold and patches of pink. They look stunning in well-lit rooms and hence make beautiful wall claddings, ﬂoorings and countertops.

Indian Juparna

Indian Juparana is found in South India. It has a pink background with black and grey veins varying in shape and sizes creating unique patterns. They can be used as kitchen countertops, bathroom vanity, wall claddings and ﬂoorings.

Moon White

Moon White Granite is a premium natural stone quarried primarily in India, celebrated for its elegant appearance and durability. This fine-grained granite features a white to light gray background adorned with intricate speckles and swirls of silvery gray, jet black, and occasional burgundy or ruby-colored garnets, giving it a distinctive and sophisticated look .

Thunder White

Thunder White Granite is a striking natural stone quarried in both Brazil and India, renowned for its captivating blend of white, gray, and subtle burgundy tones. Its intricate veining and dynamic patterns resemble stormy skies, offering a dramatic and elegant aesthetic.

Alaska Gold

Alaska Gold Granite is a luxurious natural stone celebrated for its rich golden-yellow base interlaced with intricate patterns of black, brown, and gray veins. Quarried primarily in India and Brazil, this granite is renowned for its durability, making it an excellent choice for both residential and commercial applications .

Azul White

Azul White Granite is a stunning natural stone known for its elegant white base adorned with subtle gray veining and occasional bluish specks. Quarried primarily in India, this granite is prized for its durability and resistance to scratches, stains, and heat, making it an excellent choice for high-traffic areas like kitchens and bathrooms.

Himalayan Blue

Himalayan Blue Granite is a captivating natural stone quarried primarily in India, renowned for its deep blue, gray, and brown hues interlaced with intricate wavy patterns. This granite's unique coloration and durability make it a popular choice for various interior and exterior applications, including kitchen countertops and bathroom vanities.

Chima Pink

Chima Pink Granite is a captivating natural stone quarried in the northwestern regions of India, particularly in the Jalore district of Rajasthan. Renowned for its soft pink base interspersed with specks of black, grey, and white, this granite offers a harmonious blend of warmth and elegance, making it a favored choice for various architectural and interior design applications.

Millenium Cream

Millennium Cream Granite is a versatile natural stone known for its warm, sandy beige tones interspersed with subtle veins of gold, brown, and black. Quarried in India, this granite offers a timeless aesthetic that complements both traditional and modern interiors.

Viscon White

Viscon White Granite is a striking natural stone quarried in India, renowned for its elegant white and gray background adorned with bold, swirling black and gray veins. This granite offers a dynamic and sophisticated aesthetic, making it a popular choice for both contemporary and traditional interiors.

Volga Blue

Volga Blue Granite is a striking natural stone quarried primarily in Ukraine. It features a deep black or navy blue base adorned with shimmering metallic flecks in silver, gray, and blue, creating a mesmerizing, starry-night effect. This granite is highly valued for its luxurious appearance and exceptional durability, making it a popular choice for both residential and commercial applications.

River White

River White Granite is a sophisticated natural stone quarried in India, celebrated for its elegant appearance and durability. It features a predominantly white background with soft gray veining and subtle burgundy specks, resembling the gentle flow of a river. This granite is highly regarded for its versatility and timeless appeal, making it a popular choice for various interior applications.

Kuppam Green

Kuppam Green Granite is a striking natural stone quarried in the Kuppam region of Andhra Pradesh, India. This granite is renowned for its unique blend of green, grey, and white tones, creating a sophisticated and serene aesthetic. The stone features a fine-grained texture with subtle veining, making each slab distinct and visually appealing.

Raw Silk

Raw Silk Granite is a distinctive natural stone quarried in India, celebrated for its unique coloration and patterning. This granite features a soft pink to beige base, interspersed with subtle grey and brown veining, creating an elegant and serene appearance.

Kashmir White

Kashmir White Granite is a distinguished natural stone quarried in India, renowned for its elegant and timeless appearance. It features a predominantly white to light gray background adorned with subtle gray veining and occasional burgundy speckles, creating a serene and sophisticated aesthetic.

Blue Pearl

Blue Pearl Granite is a striking natural stone renowned for its deep blue and gray tones, interspersed with shimmering silver and metallic flecks. Quarried primarily in Norway, this granite is celebrated for its durability and unique aesthetic, making it a popular choice for both residential and commercial applications.
